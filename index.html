Project Summary

International students and professionals face significant challenges when preparing for standardized English proficiency exams like IELTS and TOEFL. Traditional preparation methods often lack personalized feedback, adaptive learning paths, and comprehensive progress tracking. Many learners struggle to identify their specific weaknesses and receive timely, actionable feedback on their writing and reading skills.

BandBuddy addresses these challenges by providing an AI-powered platform that offers personalized assessment, adaptive practice sessions, and detailed progress analytics. Our target users are students and professionals preparing for IELTS or TOEFL exams who need structured, data-driven preparation support. The platform combines Google Gemini AI for intelligent essay evaluation and reading passage generation with a comprehensive dashboard that tracks user progress over time. This project is meaningful because it democratizes access to high-quality, personalized exam preparation that was previously only available through expensive tutoring services.
